Volunteering

Need volunteer hours for school? Want to help give back to the Parker community? The Parker Food Rescue is a great choice for volunteering. It provides everyone with a fun and meaningful way to give back to the community, and can even teach you about healthier lifestyles! Contact us by clicking below to become a Parker Food Rescue volunteer, helping pick up food from grocery stores, or delivering food to our recipients!

Can't Volunteer? Donate!

Donations help keep Parker Food Rescue operating, and with your donation, we'll be able to feed even more people! Please click below to our GoFundMe page, to donate!

Want to start your own food rescue?

Creating your own food rescue isn't easy, but there are tons of resources to help you along the way. The Food Rescue Alliance is an amazing organization with a mission of helping food rescue organizations through peer to peer learning, consulting, and training.
