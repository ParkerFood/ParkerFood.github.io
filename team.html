The Parker Food Rescue Team

Adit Desai- Board of Directors, Executive Director
Hi, my name is Adit Desai, and I am a junior at Legend High School. I was awake one night when a simple thought came in my head: give the food that is thrown away to the people who could use it. Of course it is not that simple. I was inspired by the Boulder and Denver Food Rescue and I wanted to start something just like that in Parker! This journey has already taught me so much and I can’t wait to see what the future holds for the Parker Food Rescue!
Andrew Lapoint-Board of Directors
My name is Andrew Lapoint and I am currently a junior at Legend High School. Currently I am involved in both FBLA and DECA at my school where I am learning how to be a businessman and that has helped guide me and prepare me for this non-profit. I decided to join my friend’s non-profit hoping to gain experience in the business world however my intentions are to help others before myself. I am trying to give back to the great community that has already given so much to me. I am very excited to be a part of such a great non-profit business and can not wait to see what is to come in my future.
