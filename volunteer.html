Volunteering Opportunities

Need any volunteer hours for your school? Or if you like giving back to your community come volunteer for the Parker Food Rescue! The Parker Food Rescue is a great choice for volunteering for it provides a fun and meaningful way to give back to the community. Help to pick up the food directly from the grocery stores and deliver it to our recipients. If you feel that delivering food is not for you, we could also use help in:

  • Graphic Design
  • Financial Expertise
  • Financial Expertise
  • Social Media Advertising

Contact us today if you are interested in becoming a volunteer for the Parker Food Rescue!
